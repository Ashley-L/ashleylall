About

Hey there, I'm Ashley. I'm a Spring 2021 graduate of the Interaction Design and Development program at George Brown College in Toronto, Ontario. I do a bit of everything - web design, brand and concept development, and a bit of graphic design as well. Currently, I'm interested in learning about everything there is to know about front-end development.

I discovered an interest in design by fluke - I had taken a digital humanities elective in my fourth year of university and to someone who spent their time cramming readings and writing essays 24/7, it was a whole new much more exciting world. For the first time in my university career, I had the opportunity to actually create something tangible (besides an essay), like an interactive game or map, and tell a story with my work.

If there's one thing you should know about me, it's that I love bubble tea. I mean - wait, it's that I'm always up for a challenge. As a relative newcomer to the world of design, I don't know everything. But - a challenge is the best way to learn something new, and that's what I'm here to do.
